VHI Limerick – Air Conditioning Installation & Builders Works

VHI Limerick Completed: 23rd March 2022

CTS Group are pleased to announce the completion of the air conditioning installation and associated builders works at VHI Limerick. The project included the installation of 16 internal wall-mounted air conditioning units, alongside electrical and mechanical works to support their operation.

Builders Works:

  • Cranage of the external unit to the roof
  • Creation of openings through walls and ceilings
  • Reinstatement of ceiling tiles
  • Fire stopping as required

Electrical Works:

  • Wiring of the external 3-phase isolator
  • Internal units and BC controllers
  • Additional breakers in the fuse board

Additional Works:

  • 2 extra air conditioning units in store room and utility room
  • Supply and installation of 2 panel heaters
  • Construction of new walls in the Canteen and Room 12

"CTS Group are delighted to have delivered this project efficiently, providing a modern and comfortable environment in full compliance with all safety and building regulations."

Sweaty Betty – Kildare Village

Unit 99, Kildare Village Completed: 17th October 2022

CTS Group completed additional Electrical Installation works at Unit 99, Kildare Village for Sweaty Betty. The scope of works was based on revised drawings and client requirements issued during the fit-out phase.

The installation included additional lighting circuits to feature light boxes and changing rooms, extra socket outlets and data points to the sales counter and back-of-house areas, including flexible conduit provisions within the floor.

Key Installations:

  • Wiring for smoke dampers and associated control panels
  • Full power and control wiring with links to the fire alarm system
  • Installation and commissioning of audio systems and people counter equipment
  • Supply and installation of front-of-house lighting fittings
  • Installation and commissioning of an AOV control panel
  • Access control installation to back-of-house doors
  • LED feature lighting around mirrors
  • Uplift to the air conditioning specification

WIT Arena – Frank & Honest Café

SETU Arena (WIT) Completed: 30th September 2022

CTS Group successfully completed the Electrical & Mechanical installation for the Frank & Honest Café at SETU Arena. The works included a series of upgrades to enhance the café's functionality and overall environment.

Key Installations:

  • Saniflo unit with plumbing and electrical wiring
  • New sink in the toilet with mixer tap
  • Upgraded communications cabinet to support CCTV
  • Relocation of the Merry Chef unit
  • Lighting alterations and switching modifications

"CTS Group is proud to have contributed to the improvement of SETU Arena's facilities and wishes the café continued success in serving its visitors."
