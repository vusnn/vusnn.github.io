WIT Arena – Frank & Honest Café

CTS Group successfully completed the Electrical & Mechanical installation for the Frank & Honest Café at SETU Arena, with the final account completed on 30th September 2022. The project involved a number of upgrades to improve both functionality and overall presentation of the café space.

Works carried out included the installation and wiring of a Saniflo unit, supply and installation of sanitary fittings, upgrades to the communications cabinet to facilitate CCTV, relocation of specialist catering equipment, and alterations to lighting and switching layouts as requested by the client.

Unit 99 Kildare Village – Sweaty Betty

CTS Group completed additional Electrical Installation works at Unit 99, Kildare Village for Sweaty Betty, with works completed on 17th October 2022. The scope of works was based on revised drawings and client requirements issued during the fit-out phase.

The installation included additional lighting circuits to feature light boxes and changing rooms, extra socket outlets and data points to the sales counter and back-of-house areas, including flexible conduit provisions within the floor. Further works included wiring for smoke dampers and associated control panels, full power and control wiring with links to the fire alarm system, and the installation and commissioning of audio systems and people counter equipment.

Additional specialist works included the supply and installation of front-of-house lighting fittings, installation and commissioning of an AOV control panel, removal of landlord fire break glass equipment, access control installation to back-of-house doors, and LED feature lighting around mirrors. An uplift to the air conditioning specification was also incorporated into the final account.
