WIT Arena – Frank & Honest Café

CTS Group successfully completed the Electrical & Mechanical installation for the Frank & Honest Café at SETU Arena, finalizing the project on 30th September 2022. The works included a series of upgrades to enhance the café’s functionality and overall environment.

Key installations included a Saniflo unit with plumbing and electrical wiring, a new sink in the toilet with a mixer tap, an upgraded communications cabinet to support CCTV, and the relocation of the Merry Chef unit. Additionally, lighting alterations and switching modifications were completed as requested by the client.
