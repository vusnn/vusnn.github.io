About CTS Group

Multi-disciplinary building services with expertise across commercial, industrial, retail and healthcare sectors

Excellence Since 1995

CTS Group are a multi-disciplinary Building services company with offices in Waterford and Dublin. We have extensive expertise in our key areas of commercial, industrial and retail and healthcare. We have also expanded in to the Healthcare, conservation and renewable fields with particular success.

We were established in 1995 and are very proud of our history and heritage in delivering a nationwide service of delivering innovative projects safely, on time and within Budget.

Our people are our most prized asset and we empower them every day to collaborate with our partners to deliver innovative solutions.
