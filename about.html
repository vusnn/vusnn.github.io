About CTS Group

A legacy of building excellence across Ireland since 1999

Our Story

Founded in Dublin in 1999, CTS Group began as a small family-owned construction business with a commitment to quality workmanship and honest relationships.

Over the past 25 years, we have grown into one of Ireland's leading construction and fit-out contractors, completing over 500 projects across retail, commercial, industrial, and public sectors.

Despite our growth, we maintain the same values that defined our early days: integrity, quality, and a relentless focus on client satisfaction.
